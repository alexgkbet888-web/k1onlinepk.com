Why can deposit and bonus show differently?

The cash balance, bonus balance, and promo tracker are not always the same section. Some pages show the deposit first, while the bonus may depend on a code, a terms condition, or a delayed promotion entry. Users should not assume the payment failed only because the bonus is missing.

Evidence to keep before the next step

Save the deposit amount, payment channel, time, and the exact screen where the bonus should appear. If a code or campaign was used, capture that too. These details matter more than repeating the deposit or assuming support will reconstruct the issue without screenshots.
