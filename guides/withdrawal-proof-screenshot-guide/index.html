What proof should show

A useful withdrawal proof screenshot should show status, time, method, and transaction reference where available. It should not expose full personal wallet details publicly.

Privacy-safe handling

FAQ

What should a withdrawal proof screenshot include?

Status, time, method, transaction reference, and matching wallet context are the most useful pieces.

Should I post wallet screenshots publicly?

No. Mask private numbers and share sensitive proof only through the proper support path.

Is one screenshot enough?

Often no. Pair wallet proof with in-app status and the time the request was made.
