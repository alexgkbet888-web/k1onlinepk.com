K-1 support and wallet help guide

Why is my K1 withdrawal pending?

A withdrawal can stay pending because bank details need review, JazzCash or Easypaisa confirmation is delayed, the account name does not match, or support still needs proof.

What proof should I keep for wallet support?

Keep transaction ID, phone number used, amount, time, wallet screenshot, status screenshot, and any support reply before retrying the same action.

Why was a deposit successful but balance not added?

The payment provider may confirm before the app balance refreshes, or the transaction may need manual review when the number, amount, or reference does not match.

Is JazzCash different from Easypaisa for K1?

Yes. Each wallet can have different confirmation timing, account-name rules, phone-number checks, and screenshot proof requirements.

When should I contact support?

Contact support when the same status remains unchanged after the normal waiting period and you already have clear proof of the transaction.
