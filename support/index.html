Before you contact support

  • Write one clear issue: wallet, login, OTP, withdrawal or bonus condition.
  • Keep one screenshot of the current state and one timestamp.
  • Do not send OTP, password or private recovery codes.
  • If the issue is wallet related, include transaction proof and balance label.

FAQ

What should I prepare before contacting K-1 support?

Prepare account ID, issue type, screenshot, timestamp, wallet or login state, and never share OTP or password.

Why is my K1 withdrawal pending?

Common checks include pending time, account eligibility, wallet label, transaction proof, bonus conditions and support evidence.

Should I send OTP to support?

No. OTP and password should not be shared. Use screenshots and issue details instead.
